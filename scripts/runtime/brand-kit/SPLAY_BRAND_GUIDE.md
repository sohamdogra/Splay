# Splay Brand Kit

Splay is the canonical brand name. Use `Splay` in prose and `SPLAY` only for compact labels where the surrounding design already establishes the name.

## Logo

The Splay mark is a five-ray fan opening from one origin. It represents context spreading into clear, actionable paths.

- `assets/splay-logo.svg` is the primary two-color mark.
- `assets/splay-logo-blue.svg`, `assets/splay-logo-charcoal.svg`, and `assets/splay-logo-white.svg` are single-color production variants.
- `assets/splay-logo-generated.png` is the original generated raster concept.
- Keep clear space equal to at least one-fifth of the mark's width.
- Do not rotate, stretch, outline, add effects, or place the mark inside another shape.

## Color

| Token | Value | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Splay Blue | `#0F5EFF` | Primary accent, mark, actions, highlights |
| Deep Blue | `#0A3DB8` | Hover and high-contrast accent |
| Charcoal | `#1F2937` | Primary text and dark surfaces |
| Ink | `#111827` | Deepest backgrounds |
| Mist | `#F3F6FA` | Soft neutral background |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Light surfaces and reversed marks |

Use blue deliberately. Most layouts should be neutral with one clear blue focal point.

## Typography

- Primary: Instrument Sans, with Inter, Arial, and sans-serif fallbacks.
- Display: Brawler may be used sparingly for editorial contrast.
- Prefer sentence case in prose and concise headings.

## Voice

Splay should sound precise, direct, useful, and evidence-led. Prefer concrete workflow behavior over abstract technology claims. Avoid hype, invented certainty, and unexplained internal jargon.

## Social creative

- Use a 16:9 canvas for feed graphics.
- Keep one strong message and generous negative space.
- Stamp the official SVG and final copy locally; do not ask an image model to redraw the logo or render final text.
- Use the blue, charcoal, mist, and white palette consistently.
- Keep the mark at least 64 px wide on a 1200 px canvas and the wordmark at least 30 px.
